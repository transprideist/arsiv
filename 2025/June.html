Istanbul Trans+ Pride Week is 11 years old this year and hoping for your support! We will hold the 11th Trans Pride Week on June 16-22 and will be culminating with a march on Sunday. This year, we come together with the theme that symbolizes resistance and the search for freedom: REBELLION.  We want to reach out to the international queer community and its allies and ask you for your solidarity with our movement in these challenging times, concerning the threatening political situation for queers and especially trans* people in Turkey. SUPPORT OUR REBELLION! Istanbul Trans+ Pride Week is about to start very soon & we need your support NOW, love! Even a small donation-5 or 10 euros-can make A real difference. If you can’t contribute financially, please consider sharing this appel. Thank you very much for your support and sharing the link to our gofundme with your community, friends, chosen families, lovers and comrades. Link in bio. In solidarity, 11th Istanbul Trans+ Pride Week Committee
